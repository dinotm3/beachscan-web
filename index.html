Smart Recommendations

Our algorithm analyzes wind, waves, water quality, UV, and temperature to generate personalized recommendations for each beach in real time.

Real-Time Weather

Live temperature, wind speed and direction, cloud cover, and precipitation from multiple verified sources. Updates every 5 minutes.

Water Quality

Official EU bathing water quality classifications. Know if the water is safe before you swim.

Find Sheltered Beaches

Looking for the best beach when it's windy? Each beach gets a wind shelter score so you can find sheltered beaches near you. No more guessing — just go where it's calm.

Plan ahead with daily beach forecasts. See which day will have the best conditions for your trip.
